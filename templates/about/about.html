About

Using machine learning for image-based disease detection in plants means using advanced technology to teach computers to spot signs of diseases in plants by analyzing images. This method is quicker and more accurate than traditional manual methods, helping farmers catch problems early and take action to protect their crops. By feeding computers lots of pictures of healthy and sick plants, they learn to recognize patterns and colors that indicate different diseases. This technology not only saves time but also helps reduce the use of harmful chemicals, making farming more sustainable in the long run.
